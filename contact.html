Frequently Asked Questions

Is MiniWorld really free?

Yes! MiniWorld is completely free for all children. We believe every child deserves access to quality educational content without any barriers.

What age is MiniWorld for?

MiniWorld is designed for children aged 4-10 years old. Our content is carefully curated to be age-appropriate and engaging for this range.

How do you keep kids safe?

We have no ads, no external links, no chat features, and no personal data collection. All content is reviewed by child development experts and educators.

Can I track my child's progress?

Yes! Parents can see their child's learning progress, achievements, and time spent on different activities through our parent dashboard.

Do you collect any personal information?

No, we don't collect any personal information from children. We're fully COPPA compliant and prioritize your child's privacy.
